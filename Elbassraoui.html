Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranderries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia apetizar topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries,and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday staster!


History:
A Brief History of Focaccia

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instan yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tabslespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubs(scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
